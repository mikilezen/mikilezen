### 🌍 Ethiopia Semantic & Recommendation Systems

A collection of projects focused on bringing advanced AI capabilities to the Ethiopian context:

- **Semantic Understanding** — NLP models and pipelines trained on Ethiopian languages (Amharic, Tigrinya, Oromo) for text classification, sentiment analysis, and information extraction.
- **Recommendation Engine** — Collaborative and content-based filtering systems tailored to Ethiopian users, supporting local content discovery and personalized suggestions.
- **Knowledge Graphs** — Structured knowledge representation for Ethiopian cultural, geographic, and domain-specific data.
